What clothes should you bring to your photo session?


This is a question we are asked everyday. After years of working with all kinds of actors we can advise you to not bring anything black. Otherwise we have broken down a what to bring list by age and stage.

Very young girls. Under 10 years old.


  • Fun colours. Pink, blue, green etc.
  • Denim jacket, or similar.
  • No thin strappy tops.
  • Try to aviod destrating patterns.
  • Chap stick!

Very young boys. Under 10 years old.


  • Fun colours, opt for shades that compliment eye colour.
  • Plain t -shirts, WHITE is most important, bring other colours too.
  • T-shirts with collars.
  • Denim jacket or similar.
  • Ties and Suit jackets are not nessessary.
  • Chap stick!

Pre-teen and Teen girls.


  • No tops with thin straps!
  • Bring a variety of tops for us to look through. Try to bring choices that are plain in design and colour. Certain prints are fine, so bring them for us to look at. Layering pieces are important too, jackets, light sweaters etc.
  • We are trying to create an all year round look so keep that in mind, no bulky sweaters!
  • No Jewellery please!
  • Chap Stick and mascara.

Pre-teen and Teen boys.


  • Shirts, opt for shades that compliment eye colour.
  • Plain t -shirts, WHITE is most important, bring other colours too.
  • T-shirts with collars.
  • Denim jacket or similar.
  • Hoodies, and sweater options.
  • Chap stick!

Adults.


  • Bring colors that pop. Avoid busy prints.
  • Bring items that you get complimented on when you go out.
  • Avoid baggy clothing
  • Wear things that fit well and compiment your shape
